# User Journey Maps

## Overview

This document maps the key user journeys for each persona, identifying touchpoints, emotions, pain points, and opportunities for improvement.

---

## Journey 1: Organized Citizen Group Leader - Campaign Mobilization (Wanjiku)

### Scenario
Wanjiku's informal traders association (500+ members) needs to respond to a tax bill that threatens their livelihoods. She has 48 hours before the bill goes to committee vote.

### Journey Stages

#### 1. Alert (Awareness)
**Touchpoint**: SMS alert on feature phone
- **Action**: Receives SMS: "URGENT: Tax bill affects traders. Reply YES for details"
- **Emotion**: 😟 Concerned (livelihood threat)
- **Expectation**: Quick understanding of threat
- **Pain Point**: SMS has character limit, needs more info
- **Opportunity**: SMS + link to mobile-optimized summary

#### 2. Understanding (Comprehension)
**Touchpoint**: Mobile web page (Swahili)
- **Action**: Opens link on 3G, reads Swahili summary
- **Emotion**: 😤 Angry (bill hurts traders) / 🤔 Strategic (how to fight)
- **Expectation**: Clear impact on informal sector
- **Pain Point**: Page loads slowly on 3G
- **Opportunity**: Aggressive caching, offline-first, skeleton screens

#### 3. Coalition Building (Organization)
**Touchpoint**: WhatsApp share feature
- **Action**: Shares bill summary to 3 trader WhatsApp groups (500+ members)
- **Emotion**: 😊 Empowered (can mobilize quickly)
- **Expectation**: Share-ready format with her commentary
- **Pain Point**: Generic share doesn't include key facts
- **Opportunity**: WhatsApp-optimized cards with bill impact + MP contacts

#### 4. Strategy (Planning)
**Touchpoint**: MP accountability section
- **Action**: Checks which MPs support traders, who's on committee
- **Emotion**: 🤔 Tactical (identify targets)
- **Expectation**: See MP voting history on trader issues
- **Pain Point**: MP data scattered, hard to find committee members
- **Opportunity**: "Target MPs" feature showing committee + voting record

#### 5. Mobilization (Action)
**Touchpoint**: Action templates
- **Action**: Downloads petition template, adapts for traders
- **Emotion**: 😊 Efficient (don't start from scratch)
- **Expectation**: Copy-paste ready petition
- **Pain Point**: Templates too formal/English
- **Opportunity**: Swahili templates, trader-specific language

#### 6. Coordination (Execution)
**Touchpoint**: Physical meeting + platform
- **Action**: Shows MP voting record at trader meeting, collects 200 signatures
- **Emotion**: 😊 Validated (data proves MP betrayal)
- **Expectation**: Printable evidence for meeting
- **Pain Point**: Can't print from phone easily
- **Opportunity**: PDF export optimized for printing at cyber cafe

#### 7. Pressure (Escalation)
**Touchpoint**: MP contact information
- **Action**: Leads delegation to MP office with petition + voting record
- **Emotion**: 😊 Confident (has evidence) / 😟 Nervous (confronting power)
- **Expectation**: MP takes them seriously
- **Pain Point**: MP dismisses them without media pressure
- **Opportunity**: Connect with journalists covering bill

#### 8. Tracking (Follow-up)
**Touchpoint**: Bill status updates
- **Action**: Monitors bill amendments, shares updates to groups
- **Emotion**: 😊 Informed (knows what's happening) / ⏰ Urgent (vote approaching)
- **Expectation**: Real-time updates on bill changes
- **Pain Point**: Updates too technical, hard to explain to members
- **Opportunity**: Plain Swahili updates: "Bill changed - now better/worse for traders"

#### 9. Victory/Defeat (Outcome)
**Touchpoint**: Final vote notification
- **Action**: Bill amended to protect informal sector (partial win)
- **Emotion**: 😊 Accomplished (advocacy worked!) / 😤 Frustrated (not full victory)
- **Expectation**: Clear explanation of what changed
- **Pain Point**: Hard to explain partial wins to members
- **Opportunity**: "What We Won" summary showing before/after

#### 10. Sustaining (Long-term)
**Touchpoint**: MP scorecard
- **Action**: Updates MP scorecard for next election
- **Emotion**: 😊 Empowered (will hold MP accountable)
- **Expectation**: Track MP promises vs actions
- **Pain Point**: Forgets details by election time
- **Opportunity**: Automated MP report card for election season

### Journey Improvements

**Quick Wins (Week 2-3)**:
1. SMS alerts with mobile-optimized links
2. Swahili-first summaries (not translations)
3. WhatsApp share cards with bill impact
4. Offline-first caching for 3G
5. Action templates in Swahili

**Medium-term (Week 4-8)**:
1. "Target MPs" feature (committee + voting record)
2. PDF export for printing at cyber cafes
3. Plain Swahili bill updates
4. "What We Won" victory summaries
5. Connect with journalists feature

**Long-term (Week 9-12)**:
1. Coalition discovery (find similar groups)
2. Automated MP scorecards for elections
3. Impact tracking (show advocacy results)
4. Audio summaries for low-literacy members
5. SMS-based participation (no data needed)

### Success Metrics
- **Mobilization**: 100+ trader groups using platform
- **Reach**: 50,000+ traders reached via WhatsApp
- **Action**: 20+ delegations to MPs per quarter
- **Impact**: 5+ bills amended to protect informal sector per year
- **Sustainability**: Groups active for 6+ months (not one-off)

## Journey 2: Investigative Journalist - Breaking Corruption Story (Sarah)

### Scenario
Sarah has 24 hours to verify and publish a story about MPs voting for a bill that benefits companies they own shares in. She needs bulletproof evidence.

### Scenario
James needs to coordinate a 6-month campaign against a controversial bill, involving 15 partner organizations and 50,000+ members across 3 counties.

### Journey Stages

#### 1. Monitoring (Awareness)
**Touchpoint**: Dashboard with tracked bills
- **Action**: Checks dashboard daily for updates
- **Emotion**: 😊 Efficient (if organized) / 😤 Frustrated (if cluttered)
- **Expectation**: Clear overview of tracked bills
- **Pain Point**: No way to organize bills into campaigns
- **Opportunity**: Add "Collections" feature for campaign organization

#### 2. Analysis (Research)
**Touchpoint**: Bill detail page with analysis tools
- **Action**: Reviews bill text, checks MP positions
- **Emotion**: 🤔 Focused / 😟 Overwhelmed (if too much data)
- **Expectation**: Quick access to key information
- **Pain Point**: MP voting records scattered across pages
- **Opportunity**: Unified analysis dashboard

#### 3. Planning (Strategy)
**Touchpoint**: Campaign planning tools
- **Action**: Creates action plan, identifies targets
- **Emotion**: 😕 Confused (feature doesn't exist)
- **Expectation**: Campaign planning workspace
- **Pain Point**: No campaign management features
- **Opportunity**: Add campaign workspace with timeline

#### 4. Mobilization (Action)
**Touchpoint**: Share and export tools
- **Action**: Exports data, shares with team
- **Emotion**: 😊 Productive (if easy) / 😤 Frustrated (if manual)
- **Expectation**: Bulk export, team collaboration
- **Pain Point**: Can only export one bill at a time
- **Opportunity**: Bulk export, shareable collections

#### 5. Coordination (Collaboration)
**Touchpoint**: Team collaboration features
- **Action**: Wants to coordinate with other advocates
- **Emotion**: 😕 Disappointed (feature doesn't exist)
- **Expectation**: Real-time collaboration
- **Pain Point**: No collaboration features
- **Opportunity**: Add shared workspaces, comments

#### 6. Measurement (Impact)
**Touchpoint**: Campaign analytics
- **Action**: Tracks campaign reach and impact
- **Emotion**: 😕 Uncertain (no metrics)
- **Expectation**: Campaign performance metrics
- **Pain Point**: No way to measure impact
- **Opportunity**: Add campaign analytics dashboard

### Journey Improvements

**Quick Wins**:
1. Add "Collections" for organizing bills
2. Bulk export functionality
3. Shareable collection links
4. Command palette shortcuts

**Long-term**:
1. Campaign workspace with timeline
2. Team collaboration features
3. Campaign analytics dashboard
4. Integration with advocacy tools

## Journey 3: Professional Advocate - Sustained Campaign (James)

### Scenario
Dr. Kamau is researching the evolution of education policy bills over the past 5 years for an academic paper.

### Journey Stages

#### 1. Research Planning (Preparation)
**Touchpoint**: Advanced search interface
- **Action**: Defines search criteria for education bills
- **Emotion**: 🤔 Focused
- **Expectation**: Powerful search with filters
- **Pain Point**: Limited historical data access
- **Opportunity**: Add date range filters, archive access

#### 2. Data Collection (Gathering)
**Touchpoint**: Search results and bill archives
- **Action**: Reviews multiple bills, takes notes
- **Emotion**: 😊 Productive (if efficient) / 😤 Tedious (if manual)
- **Expectation**: Batch operations, note-taking
- **Pain Point**: Must open each bill individually
- **Opportunity**: Multi-select, side-by-side comparison

#### 3. Analysis (Deep Dive)
**Touchpoint**: Bill analysis tools
- **Action**: Analyzes constitutional implications
- **Emotion**: 🤔 Engaged / 😕 Limited (if tools lacking)
- **Expectation**: Advanced analysis features
- **Pain Point**: No constitutional analysis tool
- **Opportunity**: Add constitutional impact analyzer

#### 4. Comparison (Synthesis)
**Touchpoint**: Bill comparison interface
- **Action**: Compares bills across years
- **Emotion**: 😕 Frustrated (feature doesn't exist)
- **Expectation**: Side-by-side comparison
- **Pain Point**: No comparison tool
- **Opportunity**: Add bill comparison feature

#### 5. Citation (Documentation)
**Touchpoint**: Export and citation tools
- **Action**: Exports data with proper citations
- **Emotion**: 😊 Satisfied (if automated) / 😤 Manual (if not)
- **Expectation**: Academic citation format
- **Pain Point**: Must manually format citations
- **Opportunity**: Auto-generate citations (APA, MLA, Chicago)

#### 6. API Access (Automation)
**Touchpoint**: API documentation
- **Action**: Uses API for programmatic access
- **Emotion**: 😊 Empowered (if available) / 😕 Limited (if not)
- **Expectation**: Well-documented API
- **Pain Point**: Limited API access
- **Opportunity**: Expand API, add Python/R libraries

### Journey Improvements

**Quick Wins**:
1. Add date range filters to search
2. Enable multi-select for bills
3. Auto-generate citations
4. Improve API documentation

**Long-term**:
1. Bill comparison tool
2. Constitutional impact analyzer
3. Historical trend analysis
4. Python/R client libraries

## Journey 4: Economic Stakeholder - Industry Lobbying (Dr. Kamau)

### Scenario
Dr. Kamau discovers a banking regulation bill at committee stage. He has 2 weeks to provide "technical input" before it goes public. The bill could cost banks KES 50B.

### Scenario
Sarah needs to quickly verify facts about a controversial bill for a story with a 2-hour deadline.

### Journey Stages

#### 1. Discovery (Alert)
**Touchpoint**: Trending bills notification
- **Action**: Receives alert about controversial bill
- **Emotion**: 😊 Informed / ⏰ Urgent
- **Expectation**: Immediate access to key facts
- **Pain Point**: Alert doesn't include summary
- **Opportunity**: Rich notifications with key facts

#### 2. Verification (Fact-Checking)
**Touchpoint**: Bill summary with sources
- **Action**: Verifies key claims about the bill
- **Emotion**: 🤔 Focused / 😟 Uncertain (if sources unclear)
- **Expectation**: Verified, quotable facts
- **Pain Point**: No clear source attribution
- **Opportunity**: Add "Verified Facts" section with sources

#### 3. Context (Background)
**Touchpoint**: Bill history and related bills
- **Action**: Researches bill background
- **Emotion**: ⏰ Rushed / 😤 Frustrated (if slow)
- **Expectation**: Quick access to context
- **Pain Point**: Related information scattered
- **Opportunity**: "Story Context" panel with timeline

#### 4. Analysis (Angle)
**Touchpoint**: MP voting records and positions
- **Action**: Identifies newsworthy angles
- **Emotion**: 😊 Productive / 😕 Limited (if data incomplete)
- **Expectation**: Complete voting records
- **Pain Point**: Some MP positions missing
- **Opportunity**: Crowdsource MP positions

#### 5. Export (Story Prep)
**Touchpoint**: Export and share tools
- **Action**: Exports timeline for article
- **Emotion**: 😊 Efficient (if easy) / 😤 Manual (if not)
- **Expectation**: Print-ready export
- **Pain Point**: Export format not print-friendly
- **Opportunity**: Add "Story Export" with timeline graphic

#### 6. Follow-up (Updates)
**Touchpoint**: Real-time updates
- **Action**: Monitors bill for breaking updates
- **Emotion**: 😊 Informed / 😟 Overwhelmed (if too frequent)
- **Expectation**: Breaking news alerts only
- **Pain Point**: Can't distinguish urgent from routine updates
- **Opportunity**: Priority-based notifications

### Journey Improvements

**Quick Wins**:
1. Rich notifications with key facts
2. "Verified Facts" section
3. Story-ready export formats
4. Priority-based notifications

**Long-term**:
1. "Story Context" panel
2. Timeline graphic generator
3. Crowdsourced MP positions
4. Press kit downloads

## Journey 5: Accessibility User - Independent Navigation (Njeri)

### Scenario
Njeri wants to research bills related to disability rights using her screen reader.

### Journey Stages

#### 1. Arrival (First Impression)
**Touchpoint**: Homepage with screen reader
- **Action**: Uses skip link to main content
- **Emotion**: 😊 Efficient (if available) / 😤 Frustrated (if not)
- **Expectation**: Skip to main content
- **Pain Point**: Skip link not always visible
- **Opportunity**: Ensure skip links on all pages

#### 2. Navigation (Orientation)
**Touchpoint**: Main navigation with keyboard
- **Action**: Navigates using heading structure
- **Emotion**: 😊 Clear (if logical) / 😕 Lost (if poor structure)
- **Expectation**: Logical heading hierarchy
- **Pain Point**: Inconsistent heading levels
- **Opportunity**: Audit and fix heading structure

#### 3. Search (Discovery)
**Touchpoint**: Search interface
- **Action**: Searches for disability-related bills
- **Emotion**: 😊 Productive (if accessible) / 😤 Blocked (if not)
- **Expectation**: Fully keyboard-accessible search
- **Pain Point**: Autocomplete breaks keyboard navigation
- **Opportunity**: Fix autocomplete keyboard support

#### 4. Reading (Comprehension)
**Touchpoint**: Bill detail page
- **Action**: Reads bill with screen reader
- **Emotion**: 😊 Clear (if well-structured) / 😕 Confused (if poor)
- **Expectation**: Clear document structure
- **Pain Point**: Tables not properly labeled
- **Opportunity**: Add table headers and captions

#### 5. Interaction (Action)
**Touchpoint**: Action buttons and forms
- **Action**: Saves bill, sets up alerts
- **Emotion**: 😊 Independent (if accessible) / 😤 Stuck (if not)
- **Expectation**: All features keyboard-accessible
- **Pain Point**: Modal dialogs trap keyboard focus
- **Opportunity**: Fix focus management in modals

#### 6. Feedback (Improvement)
**Touchpoint**: Accessibility feedback form
- **Action**: Reports accessibility issues
- **Emotion**: 😊 Heard (if responsive) / 😕 Ignored (if not)
- **Expectation**: Quick response to issues
- **Pain Point**: No dedicated accessibility contact
- **Opportunity**: Add accessibility feedback channel

### Journey Improvements

**Quick Wins**:
1. Audit and fix heading structure
2. Fix autocomplete keyboard support
3. Add table headers and captions
4. Fix modal focus management

**Long-term**:
1. Comprehensive keyboard shortcut system
2. Screen reader testing in CI/CD
3. Accessibility feedback channel
4. Regular accessibility audits

## Cross-Journey Insights

### Common Pain Points
1. **Slow Loading**: Affects Wanjiku (mobile) and all users
2. **No Collaboration**: Affects James and Sarah
3. **Limited Export**: Affects James, Dr. Kamau, and Sarah
4. **Poor Mobile Experience**: Affects Wanjiku primarily
5. **Accessibility Gaps**: Affects Njeri and others

### Common Opportunities
1. **Personalization**: Tailor experience to user type
2. **Collaboration**: Enable team features
3. **Export**: Flexible, format-rich exports
4. **Mobile Optimization**: Fast, data-efficient
5. **Accessibility**: WCAG AAA compliance

### Priority Matrix

**High Impact, Low Effort**:
- Skip links on all pages
- Rich notifications
- Bulk export
- Skeleton screens

**High Impact, High Effort**:
- Collections/campaign workspace
- Bill comparison tool
- Collaboration features
- Constitutional analyzer

**Low Impact, Low Effort**:
- Citation generator
- WhatsApp share optimization
- Story export formats

**Low Impact, High Effort**:
- Python/R client libraries
- Crowdsourced MP positions
