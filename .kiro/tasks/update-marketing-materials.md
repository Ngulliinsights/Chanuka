## Content Guidelines

### Do's ‚úÖ
- Be honest about current status
- Highlight what's working today
- Show clear roadmap
- Emphasize commitment to quality
- Use "building" or "developing" language
- Separate "Available" vs "In Development" vs "Planned"

### Don'ts ‚ùå
- Don't claim features that don't exist
- Don't inflate user numbers
- Don't claim compliance without validation
- Don't use vague terms like "comprehensive" without context
- Don't hide that we're pre-launch

### Tone
- Honest and transparent
- Confident but realistic
- Forward-looking
- Professional
- Accessible
