# Chanuka Platform User Manual

## Overview

The Chanuka Platform is a comprehensive legislative transparency tool designed to empower Kenyan citizens with access to parliamentary information, bill tracking, and civic engagement features. This manual provides step-by-step guidance for using the platform effectively.

## Getting Started

### Account Registration

1. **Access the Platform**
   - Visit the Chanuka Platform website at https://chanuka.go.ke
   - Click "Sign Up" in the top navigation

2. **Provide Required Information**
   - Email address (must be valid and accessible)
   - Secure password (minimum 8 characters with mixed case, numbers, and symbols)
   - Full name
   - Phone number (Kenyan format preferred)
   - Constituency selection

3. **Email Verification**
   - Check your email for a verification link
   - Click the link to activate your account
   - You may need to check your spam folder

### Logging In

1. Click "Sign In" in the top navigation
2. Enter your registered email and password
3. Optionally select "Remember me" for persistent login
4. Click "Sign In" to access your account

## Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you'll see the main dashboard with:

- **Recent Bills**: Latest legislative activity
- **My Activity**: Your comments and engagements
- **Notifications**: Updates on bills you're following
- **Quick Search**: Fast access to bill search
- **Constituency Focus**: Bills relevant to your area

## Browsing Bills

### Bill Categories

The platform organizes bills by status:
- **Introduced**: Newly proposed legislation
- **Committee**: Under committee review
- **Floor**: Active debate in parliament
- **Passed**: Approved legislation
- **Failed**: Rejected proposals

### Viewing Bill Details

1. Click on any bill title from the list
2. Review comprehensive information:
   - Full bill text and summary
   - Sponsor and co-sponsors
   - Current status and stage
   - Committee assignments
   - Timeline of progress
   - Related documents

### Filtering and Sorting

Use the sidebar filters to narrow results:
- **Status**: Filter by legislative stage
- **Sponsor**: View bills by specific MP
- **Committee**: Focus on specific committees
- **Date Range**: Bills from specific periods
- **Constituency**: Bills affecting your area

## Search Functionality

### Basic Search

1. Use the search bar at the top of any page
2. Enter keywords related to bill content, titles, or sponsors
3. Results highlight matching terms
4. Sort by relevance, date, or status

### Advanced Search

Access advanced search options:
- **Full-text search** across bill content
- **Boolean operators** (AND, OR, NOT)
- **Date range filtering**
- **Multiple status selection**
- **Sponsor and committee filters**

## Civic Engagement

### Commenting on Bills

1. Navigate to a bill's detail page
2. Scroll to the "Comments" section
3. Click "Add Comment"
4. Write your feedback (constructive and respectful)
5. Choose anonymity option if desired
6. Submit your comment

### Replying to Comments

1. Find a comment you want to respond to
2. Click "Reply" below the comment
3. Enter your response
4. Submit to join the discussion

### Voting on Comments

Help surface quality discussions:
- Click the upvote button on helpful comments
- Popular comments appear higher in the list

## Notifications

### Managing Notifications

1. Click the notification bell icon
2. View recent updates on:
   - Bills you're following
   - New comments on your posts
   - Legislative progress updates
   - System announcements

### Notification Preferences

Customize what you receive:
- Bill status changes
- New comments on followed bills
- Weekly digest emails
- Important announcements

## Following Bills and MPs

### Following Legislation

1. On a bill detail page, click "Follow"
2. Receive notifications on status changes
3. Access followed bills from your dashboard

### Following Members of Parliament

1. Visit an MP's profile page
2. Click "Follow" to track their legislative activity
3. Get updates on bills they sponsor

## Mobile Access

The platform is fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices:

- **Touch-friendly interface** for easy navigation
- **Offline reading** for bill content
- **Push notifications** for important updates
- **Camera integration** for document uploads

## Privacy and Security

### Data Protection

- Your personal information is encrypted and secure
- Comments can be posted anonymously
- You control your visibility preferences
- Data is never sold to third parties

### Account Security

- Use strong, unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication when available
- Log out from shared devices
- Report suspicious activity immediately

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Can't log in?**
- Verify email and password
- Check if account is verified
- Try password reset if needed

**Search not working?**
- Use simpler keywords
- Check spelling
- Try different search terms

**Comments not posting?**
- Ensure you're logged in
- Check internet connection
- Refresh the page

### Getting Help

- Use the in-app help system
- Check the FAQ section
- Contact support at support@chanuka.go.ke
- Join the community forum

## Best Practices

### Effective Engagement

- Read bills thoroughly before commenting
- Be respectful and constructive
- Use specific examples when providing feedback
- Follow bills that matter to you

### Staying Informed

- Set up email notifications for important bills
- Follow relevant committees and MPs
- Use search filters to focus on your interests
- Review the weekly digest

## Glossary

- **Bill**: A proposed law under consideration
- **Sponsor**: The MP who introduced the bill
- **Committee**: Parliamentary subcommittee reviewing bills
- **Reading**: Stages of bill consideration (First, Second, Third)
- **Constituency**: Electoral district represented by an MP
