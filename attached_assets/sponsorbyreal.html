Intelligence Weighting System

Our weighting system ensures that information about potential conflicts of interest is evaluated fairly and accurately. Several factors contribute to the weight assigned to each piece of information:

Source Credibility

Information from verified, reputable sources receives higher weighting than unverified sources.

Verification Status

Information that has been independently verified through multiple sources receives higher weighting.

Temporal Relevance

Recent information receives higher weighting than older information, with exponential decay over time.

Consistency with Existing Data

Information that aligns with multiple other sources receives higher weighting than isolated claims.

Source Anonymity Adjustment

Anonymous sources receive lower initial weighting but can increase through verification.

Domain Expertise

Information from sources with relevant expertise receives higher weighting.

Supporting Evidence

Information supported by concrete evidence (documents, recordings, etc.) receives higher weighting.
